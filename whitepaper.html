$dielaugh Whitepaper

The Die Laughing Foundation

Introduction

The Die Laughing Foundation’s $dielaugh token is a unique crypto project merging comedy, community, and charity. Our goal is to raise awareness and funds for the Penn Medicine Lung Transplant Program, while spreading joy through comedy events and content.

Our Vision

We believe that laughter and community are powerful forces for healing. $dielaugh was born out of a personal journey – created to bring people together, raise funds for a life-saving cause, and prove that crypto can make a meaningful impact beyond finance.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 tokens
  • Locked Tokens: 100,000,000 tokens
  • Utility: Donations, event access, and community voting

Why $dielaugh?

  • Supports Penn Medicine Lung Transplant Program
  • Combines humor, crypto, and charity in a new way
  • Community-driven and transparent
